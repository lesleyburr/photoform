Chicago History Museum Photo Lab

Place internal photo orders for digital files with this form. It will be directed to the Rights & Reproductions and Photo Lab staff automatically. If you are requesting collections materials, Rights & Reproductions will make sure your images are cleared for your intended use, as some of the images in our collection have restrictions. Please place you orders to allow for a two week turnaround, with the understanding that some orders make take additional time. Requests for large batches of new digital images will likely take additional time. Rush orders will be reviewed on a case-by-case basis.

If you have any questions, please feel free to email the Photo Lab or Rights & Reproductions staff.
